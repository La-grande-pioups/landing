What is Gundam?

A bit of history

Gundam is a cult franchise in Japan, it started in 1979 with Mobile Suit Gundam, a TV series that would define the codes of a new genre, the "real robot" mecha anime, which features giant robots (mobile suits) piloted by human in a futuristic but quite realistic setting. Multiple series as well as the selling of plastic model kits known as Gunpla have made this franchise cult in Japan. It has inspired many creators, especially Guillermo Del Toro, who directed Pacific Rim.
